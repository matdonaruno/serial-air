Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Serial Air ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Serial Air does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not use cookies or similar technologies
  • We do not collect device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, etc.) for tracking purposes

2. Local Network Access

Serial Air requires access to your local WiFi network to function. This access is used exclusively to:

  • Discover devices â€” The app uses mDNS (Bonjour) to find microcontroller devices broadcasting the _serial-air._tcp service on your local network
  • Connect to devices â€” The app establishes TCP connections to microcontrollers on your local network to send and receive serial data

All network communication occurs exclusively on your local network. No data is transmitted to any external server, cloud service, or third party.

3. Serial Log Data

Serial data received from your devices is:

  • Stored temporarily in app memory during your active session
  • Discarded when you close the app or clear logs
  • Optionally exported to a local file on your device when you explicitly choose to do so
  • Never transmitted to any server or third party

4. App Settings

Your preferences (font size, auto-scroll, connection settings, etc.) are stored locally on your device using standard platform storage mechanisms. These settings are:

  • Not synced to any cloud service
  • Not accessible to any third party
  • Deleted if you uninstall the app

5. Purchase Information

If you purchase the full version of Serial Air, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not receive or store your payment information, credit card details, or billing address. We receive only a confirmation that a purchase was made, which is used solely to unlock app functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

Serial Air uses the following third-party service:

  • RevenueCat â€” Used solely for in-app purchase management and receipt validation. RevenueCat may process anonymous purchase data. See their privacy policy.

Serial Air does not use any analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or social media SDKs.

7. Children's Privacy

Serial Air does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not require account creation or personal information of any kind.

8. Data Security

Since Serial Air does not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal security risk associated with the app itself. All communication occurs on your local network using standard TCP connections.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. If you have questions about how the app handles data, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
