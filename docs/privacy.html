1. Introduction

Serial Air respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our wireless serial monitor application. Your data stays on your device.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Device Connection Data

The following information is handled locally on your device:

  • IP addresses (local network only, never transmitted externally)
  • Device names discovered via mDNS
  • Device identifiers (MAC address-based)
  • Connection timestamps

2.2 Purchase Information

  • Purchase status stored locally on your device
  • All payment transactions handled entirely by Apple/Google

2.3 Local Settings

  • App preferences (display, connection defaults)
  • Trusted device list
Important

Serial Air does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data remains on your device.

3. How We Use Information

  • Connecting to devices over WiFi and Bluetooth
  • Discovering devices on your local network
  • Persisting your preferences between sessions

4. Third-Party Services

Only Apple App Store / Google Play for in-app purchases. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising SDKs.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All data stored locally via AsyncStorage
  • No cloud servers or external databases
  • All data deleted when the app is uninstalled

6. Network Communication & Security

6.1 Communication Methods

Serial Air communicates with devices using the following protocols:

  • WiFi (TCP): Unencrypted TCP socket connection over your local network. Data is transmitted in plaintext between your device and the microcontroller.
  • Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE): Nordic UART Service (NUS) over BLE. Data is transmitted using standard BLE pairing without application-level encryption.
  • mDNS: Device discovery uses multicast DNS on your local network to find compatible devices.

6.2 Security Considerations

Important

Serial Air does not encrypt serial data in transit. Any device on the same local network (WiFi) or within BLE range may potentially intercept transmitted data. Do not transmit sensitive, confidential, or personally identifiable information through serial connections.

6.3 User Responsibility

  • You are responsible for securing your own WiFi network (WPA2/WPA3 recommended)
  • Ensure physical proximity control for BLE connections
  • Do not transmit passwords, credentials, or sensitive data via serial connections
  • The Trusted Devices feature helps identify known devices but does not provide cryptographic authentication

7. Children's Privacy

Not directed at children under 13. No personal data collected from any user.

9. Changes to This Policy

Changes communicated through app updates. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

  • アプリをアンインストールするとすべてのデータが削除されます

6. ネットワーク通信とセキュリティ

6.1 通信方式

Serial Airは以下のプロトコルでデバイスと通信します：

  • WiFi（TCP）：ローカルネットワーク上の暗号化されていないTCPソケット接続。デバイスとマイクロコントローラ間のデータは平文で送受信されます。
  • Bluetooth Low Energy（BLE）：Nordic UART Service（NUS）によるBLE通信。アプリケーションレベルの暗号化なしの標準BLEペアリングを使用します。
  • mDNS：ローカルネットワーク上のマルチキャストDNSを使用してデバイスを検出します。

6.2 セキュリティに関する注意

重要

Serial Airは通信中のシリアルデータを暗号化しません。同一ローカルネットワーク上（WiFi）またはBLE通信範囲内のデバイスがデータを傍受できる可能性があります。シリアル接続を通じて機密情報、個人情報、パスワード等を送信しないでください。

6.3 ユーザーの責任

  • WiFiネットワークのセキュリティ確保はユーザーの責任です（WPA2/WPA3推奨）
  • BLE接続時は物理的な近接管理を行ってください
  • シリアル接続経由でパスワード、認証情報、機密データを送信しないでください
  • 信頼済みデバイス機能はデバイスの識別を支援しますが、暗号学的な認証は提供しません

7. お子様のプライバシー
