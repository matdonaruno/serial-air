Privacy Policy

プライバシーポリシー
Last updated: March 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Serial Air respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our wireless serial monitor application for ESP8266 and ESP32 microcontrollers. We are committed to transparency about our data practices, which can be summarized simply: your data stays on your device.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Device Connection Data

When you connect to microcontrollers, the following information is handled locally on your device:

  • IP addresses (local network only, never transmitted externally)
  • Device names discovered via mDNS
  • Device identifiers (MAC address-based)
  • Connection timestamps

All connection data is stored exclusively on your device and is never sent to any external server.

2.2 Purchase Information

  • Purchase status is stored locally on your device
  • All payment transactions are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store
  • Serial Air never receives or processes payment details

2.3 Local Settings

  • App preferences (display settings, connection defaults)
  • Trusted device list
  • All settings stored on-device via AsyncStorage
Important

Serial Air does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data remains on your device.

3. How We Use Information

3.1 Service Operation

  • Connecting to your microcontrollers over WiFi and Bluetooth
  • Discovering devices on your local network via mDNS
  • Displaying serial monitor output from connected devices

3.2 Local Storage

  • Persisting your app preferences between sessions
  • Maintaining your trusted device list for quick reconnection

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Apple App Store

In-app purchase transactions are processed through the Apple App Store. Apple's own privacy policy governs how they handle payment information. Serial Air does not receive any payment or billing details.

4.2 No Tracking Services

  • No analytics SDKs or services
  • No tracking or advertising frameworks
  • No third-party data collection of any kind

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No cloud servers or external databases are used
  • Network communication occurs only between your device and your microcontrollers on your local network
  • All app data is deleted when the app is uninstalled

6. Children's Privacy

Serial Air is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since the app does not collect personal data from any user, no special provisions for children's data are necessary.

7. International Data Transfer

Serial Air does not transfer any data internationally. All information remains stored locally on your device. There are no servers, databases, or cloud services involved in the operation of this app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through app updates on the App Store. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
