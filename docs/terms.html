2. Service Description

Serial Air is a wireless serial monitor application that enables communication with ESP8266 and ESP32 microcontrollers via WiFi (TCP/IP) and Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE). The App discovers devices on your local network using mDNS and provides a serial terminal interface for monitoring and sending data to connected microcontrollers.

  • WiFi-based serial communication over your local network
  • Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) device connectivity
  • Automatic device discovery via mDNS
  • Serial terminal with send and receive capabilities
