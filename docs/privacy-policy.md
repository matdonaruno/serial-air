## Overview

Serial Air is a wireless serial monitor app for iOS. This privacy policy explains what data Serial Air collects, uses, and shares.

## Data Collection

**Serial Air does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### Local Network Access

Serial Air uses your local WiFi network to:
- Discover microcontroller devices via mDNS/Bonjour
- Establish TCP connections to devices on your local network
- Send and receive serial data over TCP

All network communication occurs exclusively on your local network. No data is sent to external servers.

### Log Data

Serial log data received from your devices is:
- Stored temporarily in app memory during your session
- Optionally saved to local files on your device when you choose to export
- Never transmitted to any server or third party

### Settings

App settings (font size, connection preferences, etc.) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. They are not synced or transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

Serial Air does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Sharing

Serial Air does not share any data with third parties.

## Children's Privacy

Serial Air does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on the project's GitHub repository.
